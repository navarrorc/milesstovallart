MILES STOVALL

THE ARTIST

Miles Stovall is often called “The Renaissance Man” and with good reason! Miles is a multi-talented, multi-faceted individual who brings a level of excellence to everything he pursues and ultimately succeeds at, that is both impressive and inspirational to everyone who has ever met or known him!
Miles deﬁnes his philosophy of life as creating balance and well-being. He believes it is important to never forget who you truly are in this world! Miles integrates his creative, physical, mental, and social attributes into his daily life. He absolutely has a passion for all that he does and if he ﬁnds himself straying from any of his core principles that guide him, he quickly takes a step back and remembers the foundation that deﬁnes who he is as a person.
Miles was born with the heart of an artist, and his parents nurtured his pursuit of this very special talent. He studied Fine Arts at Illinois State University.
As a professional artist, Miles has had numerous art shows and he continues to paint and sell his work so others can enjoy the beauty he expresses on canvas. Living in Germany during his high school years, Miles was afforded the opportunity to absorb culture in a different light by studying art through the eyes of a European perspective. Miles credits this experience as one that helped to enlighten and enhance his gift of painting.
There is no subject matter that Miles is incapable of painting on canvas, however his strengths are deﬁnitely due to his play with light and color in both his still life and botanical paintings. Miles is also a master gardener and he incorporates his own garden into his work.
Miles expressly paints from the heart! The visions he has experienced ﬁrsthand throughout his lifetime of travel are expressed within his artwork.
To have a Miles Stovall painting is to have a gift from his very soul. It is Miles’ wish that his work will bring you joy as it has brought him while painting it!
